Designed for clarity

RidgeRelay is intentionally not “social.” It’s a focused safety companion built around planning and predictable escalation.

Overview

In low or no-cell areas, expectations matter more than connectivity. RidgeRelay centers safety around clear intent, return timing, and escalation guardrails.

What RidgeRelay prioritizes

  • Planning over posting
  • Clarity over complexity
  • Predictable escalation
  • Offline-aware mindset

How it works

  • 🧭
    Plan Declare intent and return time.
  • 🧱
    Guardrails Set boundaries and expectations.
  • ⏱️
    Check-ins Choose a cadence that fits conditions.
  • 🚨
    Escalation Trigger progressive steps if needed.
