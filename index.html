Overview

Out here, your phone can go from “fine” to “no service” in minutes. That’s normal — but it makes it harder for others to know your plan, your boundaries, and when to worry.

RidgeRelay is a concept for a lightweight outdoor safety system built around planning: intent, timing, and check-in rhythm. If check-ins stop, escalation follows clear steps.

What RidgeRelay prioritizes

  • Planning over posting
  • Clarity over complexity
  • Trust through predictable steps
  • Offline-aware mindset (concept)

How it works

A simple 4-step flow — built to feel calm under stress.

  • 🧭
    Plan Declare intent, start time, and expected return.
  • 🧱
    Guardrails Set boundaries so someone else understands the plan.
  • ⏱️
    Check-ins Choose a cadence that matches conditions.
  • 🚨
    Escalation Progressive steps if you miss check-ins — no surprises.
