How RidgeRelay works

Safety-first flows that stay calm, private, and session-limited.

1) Declare intent

Start a safety session with your plan: who, where, and when you’ll be back.

2) Guardrails

Set boundaries and expectations (time windows, check-ins, and low-signal awareness).

3) Nudges

Helpful prompts before anything escalates — designed to reduce false alarms.

4) Escalate only if needed

If the session goes quiet past your rules, RidgeRelay can notify trusted contacts.

FAQ

Short answers for the prototype.

Is this live tracking?

No. This is a concept demo: no real GPS, no dispatch, no live monitoring.

What does “privacy-first” mean here?

Session-limited data, clear boundaries, and “share only when needed” escalation design.

Can I use this in the backcountry today?

Not yet. Treat this as a UI prototype to communicate the concept and flow.
