FAQ

Short answers that reduce confusion and build trust.

Is this real tracking?

No — this is a prototype. Buttons simulate states to demonstrate intent-aware safety UX.

Does RidgeRelay replace AllTrails?

No — it complements trail discovery with an optional safety layer for low/no-signal scenarios.

Do I have to do extra steps to stop tracking?

In the concept, no — the session auto-ends when you finish or return, and you get a clear confirmation.
